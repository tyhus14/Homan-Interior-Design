About

I’ve always wanted three things from my own home: practicality, comfort, and low-maintenance living. With 27 years of interior design, renovation, and remodeling experience, I want you to enjoy those same luxuries. I offer a style that lies somewhere between a “do-it-yourself” job and a “decorator show house.” While I will give you my honest, professional opinion, you always have the final say. To make your project a successful one, we will discuss only those options that suit your needs. It’s your home. Feel at home in it.
